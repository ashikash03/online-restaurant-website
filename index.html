About

Since opening our doors in 2000, our restaurant has become a beloved culinary destination in the vibrant cities of Kochi, Kozhikode, and Trivandrum. We are proud to house a diverse array of flavors from across Kerala and around the world, offering a unique dining experience that celebrates both traditional and contemporary cuisine. Our team is led by a group of well-known chefs, each bringing their own expertise and creativity to the table. Their passion for food and commitment to excellence ensure that every dish is crafted with the utmost care and attention to detail. Whether you're indulging in our signature Kerala dishes or exploring our international offerings, you'll find that every bite is a testament to our dedication to quality and taste. Join us for an unforgettable culinary journey that promises to delight your senses and leave you coming back for more.
